Christmas Gifts

The magi were three wise men who brought gifts to Baby Jesus in the manger in a biblical story of Christmas.
It is from there that the art of giving Christmas presents was invented.


Where did giving gifts for Christmas originate?

Gift-giving in general is an ancient tradition that came to be associated with the Christian feast of Christmas.
In ancient Rome, gift giving might have occurred near the winter solstice in December which was celebrated during the Saturnalia holiday.

When did the tradition of giving gifts at Christmas begin??

Although such efforts did contribute to the magnitude of the ritual, the practice of buying Christmas presents for children predates the spread of corporate capitalism in the United States.
It began during the first half of the 1800s, particularly in New York City, and was part of a broader transformation of Christmas.
