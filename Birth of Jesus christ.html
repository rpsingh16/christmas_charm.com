Birth of Jesus Christ

Story of Jesus Christ Birth

Mary, a young teenager, was living in the village of Nazareth and engaged to be married to Joseph, a Jewish carpenter. One day God sent the angel Gabriel to visit Mary.
The angel told Mary that she would conceive a son by the power of the Holy Spirit. She would give birth to this child and name him Jesus.

When was Jesus actually born according to the Bible?

The date of birth of Jesus is not stated in the gospels or in any historical reference,
but most biblical scholars assume a year of birth between 6 and 4 BC.
